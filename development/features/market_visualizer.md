## Description
This feature provides an analytical dashboard to analyze pricing clusters and deals scraped from secondary markets. It plots item listings against historical distributions to flag undervalued deals.

### Capabilities
- **Scatter Telemetry Grid**: Plots pricing vs vehicle mileage/year, highlighting outliers in deep green (great value) or soft orange.
- **K-Means Market Clustering**: Segments listings into pricing groups based on mileage, wear indicators, and features.
- **Live Deal Feeds**: Displays an interactive feed of the latest scraped items filtered by calculated margin indices.
